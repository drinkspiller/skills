# Platform Constraints

Character limits and format rules per platform. When generating multi-platform
variants, write *natively* for each — don't truncate.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Search Ads

### Google Ads (Responsive Search)

Element          | Limit         | Notes
---------------- | ------------- | ---------------------------------------------
Headline         | 30 chars each | Up to 15 headlines. Google mixes and matches.
Description      | 90 chars each | Up to 4 descriptions.
Display URL path | 15 chars each | 2 path fields. Use for keywords.

**Tips**: Front-load the value. Include the keyword naturally. Each headline
must work independently — they'll be shuffled. No exclamation marks in
headlines.

### Microsoft Ads

Same limits as Google Ads. Audience skews slightly older and more desktop-heavy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Social Ads

### Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

Element            | Limit         | Notes
------------------ | ------------- | ---------------------------------------
Primary text       | 125 chars     | Visible before "See more." Total: 2200.
Headline           | 40 chars      | Below the image/video.
Link description   | 25 chars      | Below the headline.
Image text overlay | < 20% of area | More text = less distribution.

**Tips**: The first sentence IS the ad. Everything after "See more" is bonus.
Write the primary text as if "See more" doesn't exist.

### Instagram (Organic / Boosted)

Element       | Limit      | Notes
------------- | ---------- | -----------------------------------------
Caption       | 125 chars  | Visible before truncation. Total: 2200.
Story text    | ~120 chars | Must be readable over visual. Large type.
Reels caption | 90 chars   | Auto-truncated. Front-load.

### X (Twitter)

Element   | Limit     | Notes
--------- | --------- | -----------------------------------------------
Post text | 280 chars | Promoted posts same limit. Links use ~23 chars.
Ad copy   | 280 chars | Images/video don't count against limit.

**Tips**: Brevity is the whole game. One idea per post. Threads for longer
narratives.

### LinkedIn

Element     | Limit     | Notes
----------- | --------- | --------------------------------------
Intro text  | 150 chars | Visible before "see more." Total: 600.
Headline    | 70 chars  | Below the image. 200 max.
Description | 100 chars | InMail: 200 chars for CTA.

**Tips**: Professional doesn't mean boring. LinkedIn audiences respond to
directness and data. Lead with a specific number or contrarian claim.

### TikTok

Element      | Limit     | Notes
------------ | --------- | ----------------
Ad text      | 100 chars | Above the video.
Display name | 40 chars  | Brand name.

**Tips**: Don't write ads. Write captions that belong on the platform. If it
reads like marketing, it dies in the feed.

## Display & Video

### YouTube

Element   | Limit      | Notes
--------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------
In-stream | :05 bumper | 5 seconds. One idea. One line.
In-stream | :15 / :30  | Hook in first 5 seconds or lose them.
Discovery | 25+35+35   | Headline (25) + Description lines (35 each).
Companion | 300x60 px  | Banner. 2-4 words maximum.

### Display (GDN / Programmatic)

Format       | Headline  | Description | Notes
------------ | --------- | ----------- | -----------------------------
Banner (all) | ~25 chars | ~35 chars   | Must work at 300x250 minimum.
Native       | 25 chars  | 90 chars    | Blend with editorial content.

**Tips**: Display is drive-by. Billboard rules apply — understood at a glance or
not at all.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Email

Element      | Limit       | Notes
------------ | ----------- | -------------------------------------
Subject line | 40-50 chars | 30-40 ideal for mobile preview.
Preview text | 40-90 chars | Complements subject. Don't repeat it.
CTA button   | 2-5 words   | Verb + outcome. "Get my report."

**Tips**: The subject line is the ad. If it doesn't get opened, nothing else
matters. Preview text is your second headline — use it.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Out of Home

Element   | Limit       | Notes
--------- | ----------- | ---------------------------
Billboard | 7 words     | Industry standard maximum.
Transit   | 10-12 words | Slightly more time to read.
Poster    | 15-20 words | Closer reading distance.

**Tips**: If you can't read it at 65mph, it's too long. One image, one message,
one CTA (if any). Some of the best OOH has no CTA at all — just a brand mark.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## General Platform Rules

1.  **Never truncate — rewrite.** A 280-char tweet shortened to 125 chars for
    Instagram is not an Instagram ad. It's a mutilated tweet.
2.  **Platform-native voice.** LinkedIn can be smarter. TikTok must be looser.
    Email can be longer. Billboard must be shortest.
3.  **CTA adapts to platform.** "Learn more" on LinkedIn. "Shop now" on Meta.
    "Swipe up" on Stories. "Try free" on search.
4.  **Mobile-first.** Most impressions are mobile. If it doesn't work on a phone
    screen, it doesn't work.
