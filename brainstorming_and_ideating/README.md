# Brainstorming & Ideating Skill Package (`brainstorming-and-ideating`)

This skill package explores requirements, mines consumer insights and tensions,
generates structured architectural or creative concepts, and evaluates proposals
against multi-axis rubrics before implementation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Upstream Attribution

This skill synthesizes foundational methodologies, cognitive frameworks, and
structural constraints from the following internal and open-source repositories.
Every instruction, mining sequence, and scoring rubric has been rewritten in
original language:

*   **`gpowers` / `obra/superpowers`** (Jesse Vincent):
    [github.com/obra/superpowers](https://github.com/obra/superpowers/tree/main/skills/brainstorming)
    *   *Core Influence*: Single-Question Policy, bite-sized incremental
        validation blocks (200 to 300 words), YAGNI simplification, and
        project-native architectural trade-offs.
*   **`creative-director`**:
    [creative_director/SKILL.md](../creative_director/SKILL.md)
    *   *Core Influence*: Mark Pollard's Four Points (Problem → Insight →
        Advantage → Strategy), tension spotting (Cultural/Category/Human),
        Simplicity as Violence ("if an idea cannot be explained in one sentence,
        it is a plan"), warmup pruning (serial order effect), competitor swap
        testing, multi-axis scoring rubrics, and Kill Your Darlings
        stress-testing.
*   **`alexei-led/cc-thingz` & `romiluz13/cc-p4p`**:
    [alexei-led/cc-thingz](https://github.com/alexei-led/cc-thingz/blob/main/src/skills/brainstorming-ideas/SKILL.md)
    &
    [romiluz13/cc-p4p](https://github.com/romiluz13/cc-p4p/blob/main/plugins/cc-p4p/skills/pm-brainstorming/SKILL.md)
    *   *Core Influence*: Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) grounding, target audience pain
        point mapping, and architectural trade-off exploration.
*   **`Orchestra-Research/AI-Research-SKILLs` & `as4584/antigravity-skills`**:
    [Orchestra-Research/AI-Research-SKILLs](https://github.com/Orchestra-Research/AI-Research-SKILLs/blob/main/21-research-ideation/brainstorming-research-ideas/SKILL.md)
    &
    [as4584/antigravity-skills](https://github.com/as4584/antigravity-skills/blob/main/skills/brainstorming/SKILL.md)
    *   *Core Influence*: Empirical verifiability, quantifiable hypothesis
        framing, and falsifiable success criteria.
*   **`phuryn/pm-skills` & `lobehub`**:
    [phuryn/pm-skills](https://github.com/phuryn/pm-skills/blob/main/pm-product-discovery/commands/brainstorm.md)
    &
    [lobehub](https://lobehub.com/skills/obra-superpowers-brainstorming?version=1.0.2&activeTab=skill)
